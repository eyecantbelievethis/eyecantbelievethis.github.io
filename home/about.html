About


This website was developed for an easy and convenient way to access Tiny Terrors transcripts.

All episode descriptions are taken from Spotify, and all transcripts marked [U] are unofficial and fan-transcribed, with the transcriber linked at the top of the page.


This site will never post or transcribe anything from the Pulp Audio Patreon.

If you want access to that content, please support them directly by subscribing to their Patreon.



A bit about me -- my name is Cas, and I'm the main person working on this project! If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions, don't hesitate to DM me on Tumblr @eyecantbelievethis or email me at eyecantbelievethis@gmail.com.


Special thanks to Snarp who made The Magnus Archives Transcripts Archive Archive A, which is a big inspiration for this website.




The transcripts featured are fan-transcribed, and may not be accurate.
