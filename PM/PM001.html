Okay, okay, okay, D. Like what?

Well, for starters? A release schedule.

A release schedule.

We do.

We do.

I mean we could not have, a release, schedule?

Or we could just figure out a release schedule before I throw you both through the wall headfirst. We-we-we ask the audience to rate and review - we have to be consistent on our end. * Transcriber's Note: On October 12, 2022, an ARG for another podcast called The Magnus Archives was in full swing. Due to some suspicious tweets and a code found in the Episode Description from TT009, TT010, and TT011, fans have speculated that choosing this day to release episodes was not a coincidence. As of November 11th, 2022, the connection between Tiny Terrors and The Magnus Archives is purely fan speculation.
